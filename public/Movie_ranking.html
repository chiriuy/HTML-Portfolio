The Best Movies According to Christian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Last Samurai

Great Movie, It's basically Dances with Wolves in Feudal Japan but it just scratches an itch I cannot describe

Kingdom of Heaven

War, Honor, Sacrifice and Duty. Excellent movie, fits my taste juuust right

The Edge of Tomorrow

Sci-Fi, good script, good acting and an interesting take on the "alien invaders" trope
